# Using the mixer
- To set the high-pass filter
    - Select the Edit button at the top of the screen
    - Select the Red EQ tab just under the edit button
    - Select the channel tab at the bottom of the screen
    - Drag blue low filter to the right until the -20 crossing passes at 50hz
- To set the recording volumes:
    - Center all three knobs on the physical mixer to high noon
    - Ignore the gradiations and only pay attention to the blue numbers
    - Select Mix on the Mix/Gain button
    - Set the faders on the first three channels to 0.0db (-24) by right clicking on the blue numbers
    - Mute the unused channels by clicking each mute button to red
    - Toggle Mix/Gain to Gain (red)
    - Play back a known signal into each channel in turn
        - Set the gain level slider until the channel level lights peak at -24
